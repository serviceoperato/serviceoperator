Patient Journey Analysis

Five-stage funnel from discovery to follow-up. Each stage includes friction points identified through public data analysis and operational flow mapping.

Stage 1
🔍 Discovery
Functional

Status: Ranks organically for "medical clinic Bangkok" and wellness-related queries. Strong local presence.

  • No paid search strategy detected — organic-only creates CAC ceiling
  • Limited service line keywords (medical vs beauty vs wellness treated separately)
Stage 2
💬 First Contact
Critical Gap

Status: No live chat, no WhatsApp integration, email only — creates 6-24 hour response lag.

  • No instant communication widget on website
  • WhatsApp Business account not linked to booking system
  • No LINE ID on homepage (critical for Thai audience)
  • No chatbot for after-hours international inquiries
Stage 3
📋 Consultation Booking
Manual Process

Status: Free consultation form exists but no stated response SLA. Manual intake requires staff review.

  • No instant booking confirmation (patients wait for staff callback)
  • International patients face timezone delays (US/EU inquiries get 18-hour wait)
  • No service line routing (all inquiries hit same inbox)
  • No pre-qualification questions (wasted intake time on wrong service matches)
Stage 4
📅 Appointment Confirmation
Critical Gap

Status: No automated confirmations, reminders, or rescheduling. Manual SMS/email only.

  • No appointment confirmation system detected
  • No automated reminders (estimated 22% no-show rate)
  • No self-service rescheduling — patients must call
  • No pre-visit checklist (delays treatment intake)
Stage 5
⭐ Post-Visit Follow-Up
Partial

Status: No automated review request sequence detected. Follow-ups are ad-hoc.

  • No automated post-visit survey or review nudge
  • No follow-up appointment reminder sequence
  • No patient education content sent post-visit
  • Review response lag: 18-25 days average (industry: 3-5 days)
