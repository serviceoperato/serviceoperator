## 3. INTAKE & BOOKING FRICTION

### ⚠️ HIGH FRICTION POINTS

**1. No Real-Time Booking Widget**  
Patients must email or call to book. For international patients researching across multiple clinics at midnight in Australia, a competitor with instant booking wins the slot.

**2. Free Consultation Form → Unknown Response Time**  
The "Free Online Consultation" page has a form but no stated response time, no auto-reply confirmation, and no chatbot backup. A patient who submits at 11pm Thailand time may not hear back for 12–18 hours.

**3. No WhatsApp / LINE Button on Homepage**  
DDC's contact page lists phone numbers and email. However, no prominent WhatsApp click-to-chat or LINE QR code is visible on the homepage or main navigation. In Thailand's dental tourism market, LINE is the default async communication tool. Its absence is a conversion killer.

**4. Pricing Page Exists but Lacks Instant Estimate Tool**  
Fees page is live but requires manual review. No interactive cost calculator (e.g., "How much for 4 veneers + whitening?") exists to pre-qualify high-value patients.

**5. Multi-Step Contact for International Patients**  
International patients face: discover → read → form-fill → wait for email → schedule call → book. Each step is a drop-off point with no automation bridging the gaps.

### Top 3 AI Systems for DDC

**System 1 — AI Patient Intake Bot (LINE / WhatsApp / Email)**  
An AI trained on DDC's service menu, pricing, FAQs, and booking workflow. Responds in English, Thai, or Chinese within 60 seconds, 24/7. Collects: name, country, service interest, travel dates, dental photos if applicable. Routes to human staff for confirmation only when ready. Estimated inquiry capture rate improvement: 40–60% (based on industry benchmarks for similar dental tourism operations).

**System 2 — Post-Treatment Review Sequence**  
Automated 3-message sequence: Day 1 (thank you + care instructions), Day 7 (check-in + Google review request), Day 30 (satisfaction + referral ask). Sent via LINE (Thai patients), WhatsApp (international), or email. Each message is personalized by procedure type. Targets 5-star Google review generation at scale.

**System 3 — Booking Confirmation + Reminder Stack**  
Instant booking confirmation (within 2 minutes of form submit), 7-day reminder with travel prep tips, 24-hour reminder with clinic address + parking info. Eliminates no-shows and builds pre-visit confidence for first-time international patients.
