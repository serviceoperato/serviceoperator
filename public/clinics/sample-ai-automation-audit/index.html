Patient journey audit

🗺️
Narrative (synthetic)
Discovery through search and referrals works, but digital handoff to a confirmed chair is where the composite clinic loses speed. Below is a representative six-stage map used in real audits.
Stage 1 — Discovery
✓ Functional
  • Organic visibility for high-intent keywords (illustrative)
  • Limited structured data / FAQ for rich results (example gap)
Stage 2 — First contact
⚠ High friction
  • No single “preferred channel” CTA above the fold
  • After-hours inquiries queue until the next business day
Stage 3 — Triage & quote
⚠ Manual
  • Treatment estimates require coordinator back-and-forth
  • No templated reply pack for top five procedures
Stage 4 — Booking
⚠ Critical gap
  • No self-serve slot selection (phone / form loop only)
  • Confirmation messages not standardized across channels
Stage 5 — Pre-visit
⚠ Missing
  • Reminder cadence inconsistent for international arrivals
  • Intake paperwork not prefilled from prior chat
Stage 6 — Post-visit
⚠ Partial
  • Happy patients rarely receive a structured review ask
  • No lightweight “day two” check-in for high-value cases
