01

Context

Lumina is portrayed as a four-chair-group brand doing veneers, implants, and smile design for tourists and expats. Each branch ran its own LINE Official Account, two shared WhatsApp numbers, and a central Gmail — with no shared SLA, no quote library, and weekend coverage only at Sukhumvit.

Leadership asked ServiceOpera for a 48-hour private audit, then a 90-day pilot focused on pre-visit operations (not clinical protocols): faster replies, fewer no-shows, more reviews, shorter quote cycles.

02

The problem

⚠
Audit finding (fictional)
Revenue was not lost in the chair — it leaked between first message and confirmed consult, especially Friday–Sunday AU inquiries and implant quotes waiting on photos.
  • ✗Median first response 4h 20m (weekends often >12h on non-Sukhumvit branches)
  • ✗Inquiry → booked consult conversion stuck at 11.2% despite strong clinical reputation
  • ✗Implant consult no-shows at 18.6% with single SMS reminder only
  • ✗Treatment quotes averaged 36 hours — competitors in audit bench replied in <12h
  • ✗Only 28 new Google reviews/month group-wide; negative themes cited “slow WhatsApp”
03

ServiceOpera intervention

Work stayed in operations tooling the group already used (LINE, WhatsApp Business, Google Sheets CRM). No EMR replacement, no clinical AI on diagnostics.

  • ✓Unified triage inbox with branch tags, language detection, and human escalation rules
  • ✓Instant acknowledgement + FAQ snippets (EN / TH / simplified CN) within 60 seconds, 24/7
  • ✓3-touch reminder stack for implant consults (T−72h, T−24h, day-of)
  • ✓Quote accelerator — photo checklist + templated ranges by procedure tier
  • ✓Post-visit review sequence at T+48h with owner digest every Monday
  • ✓Weekly ops retro — conversion, response SLA, no-shows, review velocity

90-day timeline

Days 0–7 · Audit & baselines

48-hour written brief; channel map; manual conversion spreadsheet; SLA snapshot per branch.

Days 8–21 · Quick wins

Instant ack on all channels; bilingual FAQ; pinned WhatsApp/LINE on mobile; first quote PDF pack for top 3 procedures.

Days 22–45 · Core workflows

Unified triage; 3-touch reminders live on implant consults; review ask at T+48h; weekly owner digest.

Days 46–90 · Scale & reactivation

Quote templates by tier; abandoned-quote nudges; Phuket/Chiang Mai weekend coverage parity; ops retro tied to KPI table above.
