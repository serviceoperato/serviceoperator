# Website section copy

## Section 1 — Hook

Pattaya clinics do not lose money because they lack demand. They lose money because inquiries arrive from too many channels and are not converted into a clean pipeline.

## Section 2 — Public footprint audit

For SEA Clinic, public data shows strong treatment positioning, doctor-led trust markers, review assets and high-margin services. The weak point is not reputation. The weak point is operational capture after the first message.

## Section 3 — Automation diagnosis

A lead asking about Botox, fillers or Ulthera should not disappear inside WhatsApp. It should become:

- treatment interest
- urgency
- preferred language
- target area
- budget band
- desired appointment date
- follow-up status
- review/rebooking trigger

## Section 4 — Proposed system

Build a quiet AI operator behind the clinic:

1. WhatsApp + LINE concierge
2. Treatment qualification
3. Booking handoff
4. Review recovery
5. Repeat-treatment reminders
6. Competitor price radar

## Section 5 — CTA

If this was built from public data, imagine what can be built from your real inquiries.
