Positive themes

  • Physician thoroughness and perceived clinical quality.
  • Facility cleanliness and wayfinding for international patients.
  • Value framing versus home-country pricing for elective procedures.

Friction themes

  • Inconsistent deposit and refund communication across channels.
  • Delays when moving from LINE to phone verification.
  • Package add-ons not explained in the same language as the initial inquiry.
