### Automation Detail

**1. WhatsApp Pre-Arrival Flow**
Trigger: 48h before check-in. Message: "Welcome to Meliá Pattaya — your room is ready. Add a YHI Spa treatment for 20% off. Book your beach club daybed. Arrange airport transfer." 3-message sequence. Estimated additional revenue: THB 800–2,500 per guest per stay.

**2. AI Review Response Engine**
Deploy AI to draft and (with approval) post responses across Google, TripAdvisor, Booking.com, Agoda, Trip.com within 24h. Personalised to review content. Include return-visit offer code in negative review responses. Reduces Guest Experience Manager manual workload by ~80%.

**3. Post-Checkout Review Solicitation**
Trigger: 2h after checkout. WhatsApp or email: "How was your stay? Share your experience on Google / TripAdvisor." Direct link. Guest who rates 4+ is directed to Google. Guest who rates 3 or below is directed to internal feedback form. Estimated Google review velocity increase: 40–70%.

**4. LINE OA (Official Account) Setup**
Thailand has ~52M LINE users. Pattaya's primary domestic guest market uses LINE daily. A LINE OA enables: booking inquiries, pre-arrival info, upsell broadcast, and post-stay follow-up — all in the channel guests already live in.

**5. Spa & F&B Upsell Broadcast**
Tuesday and Thursday push: "Join us tonight at Lay Beach Club. Hotel guests receive priority access." Estimated conversion: 5–15% open rate on WhatsApp, 3–8% on LINE. Incremental F&B revenue per month.

**6. Mid-Stay Satisfaction Recovery**
Trigger: Day 2 of stay (for stays ≥2 nights). "How's your stay going? Let us know if anything can be improved." Internal alert for any score below 4/5. Enables real-time recovery before guest leaves and posts publicly.
