Case Study

How SmileCare Dental Studio Found €380K In Hidden Revenue

A 15-year-old award-winning dental clinic in Milan was leaking revenue every single day. An AI audit revealed exactly where, and how to fix it in 21 days.

The Problem: Invisible Leaks

SmileCare had a stellar reputation. International clientele. Premium positioning. But something was broken in the machinery—and nobody could see it.

  • No live chat on website — Inquiries from 2 AM to 7 AM went unanswered (international patients, different timezones)
  • No instant booking confirmation — Patients waited 24-48 hours for phone/email response. Many booked elsewhere
  • No automated appointment reminders — 18% no-show rate. Lost chair time. Lost revenue
  • Manual consultation forms — Data entry took 3-4 hours per day. Delayed consultations
  • No follow-up system — Interested prospects fell through the cracks after first contact
Result: €380,000 in annual revenue leaking through the cracks.

The clinic wasn't aware of it. They had patients, revenue was growing, everything looked fine from the outside. But the internal machinery was running at 60% efficiency.

The Solution: 21-Day Automation Sprint

Instead of a 6-month implementation, everything was automated in three weeks.

Audit & Setup

Days 8-14

Optimization
Team training. Response templates refined. Performance metrics baseline established.
